About the program

  • Establishes peer-led groups to help first-generation students connect and build a strong support network
  • Hosts regular events that bring first-generation students together, fostering a sense of unity and shared identity
  • Provides dedicated spaces on campus where first-generation students can gather, collaborate, and relax
  • Offers scholarships and financial literacy programs to help first-generation students manage college costs and plan for the future

Value

  • First-generation scholars bring unique experiences that enrich discussions and promote a more inclusive campus culture
  • Ability to overcome challenges inspires and motivates others to persevere in difficult situations
  • Fostering a connected and supportive community
  • Welcoming and supportive for all students
  • Building initiatives that strengthen student bonds
